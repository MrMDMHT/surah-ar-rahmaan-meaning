surah rahman meaning

Surah Ar-Rahman is the 55th chapter of the Quran, containing 78 verses .012 Its title means "The Beneficent" in English, and it lists many blessings and favors that Allah has bestowed on us. The ayat "Fabi ayyi aalaaa'i Rabbikumaa tukazzibaan" is repeated 31 times throughout the Surah, which translates to "Then which of your Lord's blessings would you both deny?" Surah Ar-Rahman states the gifts of Allah that have been given to this world, to us. According to a hadith, Muhammad () said, "Everything has an adornment, and the adornment of the Qur"an is Surah Ar-Rahman."
